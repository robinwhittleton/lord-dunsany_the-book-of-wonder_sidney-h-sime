Colophon

The Book of Wonder
was published in 1912 by
Edward John Moreton Drax Plunkett, 18th Baron of Dunsany
and illustrated by
Sidney Herbert Sime.

This ebook is based on a transcription produced in 2010 by
Suzanne Shell, Charles Bidwell, and The Online Distributed Proofreading Team
for
Project Gutenberg,
and on digital scans available at the
Internet Archive.

The cover page is adapted from
View of Tiflis,
an illustration completed in 1868 by
Ivan Konstantinovich Aivazovsky.
The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.
